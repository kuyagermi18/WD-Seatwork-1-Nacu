The Stoic Sorcerer

Megumi Fushiguro is a calm, collected, and incredibly powerful jujutsu sorcerer. Known for his shikigami summoning technique, he believes in saving good people even if it costs his life — a belief inherited from his mentor Gojo Satoru.

“I want more people to be saved fairly... without anyone deciding who deserves it.” – Megumi
