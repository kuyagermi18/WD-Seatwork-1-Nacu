The Special Grade Sorcerer

Yuta Okkotsu is not your average sorcerer. Once consumed by grief and fear, he has transformed into one of Jujutsu High’s most powerful allies. He shares a deep bond with Rika, a cursed spirit who remained with him even after death.

Despite his power, Yuta remains soft-spoken and kind — a reminder that strength doesn’t always roar. His curse is his strength, and his heart is his weapon.

“If I can curse someone... I can also protect someone.”
