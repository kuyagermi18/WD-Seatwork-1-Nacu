The Vessel of Sukuna

Itadori Yuuji is not just an ordinary high school student. Despite being thrown into the terrifying world of curses, he remains grounded in his strong sense of justice. After swallowing Sukuna’s finger, he became the host of the King of Curses — yet he fights to protect others with unmatched determination.

“I don’t know how I’ll feel when I’m dead. But I don’t want to regret the way I lived.” – Itadori
